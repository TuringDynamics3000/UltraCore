## Recommendation: Stick with 4 Pods

### ‚úÖ Reasons to Keep 4 Pods

1. **Complete Coverage** - 100% of common goals covered
2. **Simple Mental Model** - Easy for customers to understand
3. **Distinct Purposes** - Each pod has clear objective
4. **Scalable** - Can handle multiple goals per pod
5. **Efficient** - Master optimization works best with 4 pods
6. **Proven Framework** - Aligns with academic research (Shefrin & Statman)

### ‚ùå Reasons NOT to Add More Pods

1. **Complexity** - More pods = harder to explain to customers
2. **Overlap** - Additional pods would overlap with existing ones
3. **Optimization Difficulty** - Master portfolio optimization harder with 5+ pods
4. **Diminishing Returns** - Marginal benefit of 5th pod is low
5. **Trading Costs** - More pods = more ETFs = higher costs
